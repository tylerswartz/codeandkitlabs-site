Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Code & Kit Labs ("we," "our," or "us") provides AI-powered communication services for local businesses.

Information We Collect

When you text a business using our service, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • Message content
  • Date and time of messages

How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about business services, hours, and scheduling
  • Improve our service quality
  • Comply with legal requirements

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your messages are only visible to the business you contacted and our service providers (Twilio, Anthropic) who help deliver the service.

Data Retention

Conversation history is stored for up to 30 days to provide continuity in multi-message conversations, then automatically deleted.

Your Rights

You can opt out of messages at any time by replying STOP. For questions about your data, contact: legal@codeandkit.com

SMS Communications

Mobile information will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.
