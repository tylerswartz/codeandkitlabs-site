Origin
I help Seattle businesses
make more money with AI.

Most small business owners know they should be using AI. Almost none of them are — not because they don't want to, but because figuring out what to actually build and how to build it is a full-time job on top of the full-time job they already have.

I come in, learn how your business actually operates, and build AI tools that either bring in more revenue or cut down the work that's eating your time. Customer communications, scheduling, follow-ups, back-office automation — whatever the biggest drain is, that's where we start.

I do this as someone currently running a service business in Seattle, not as a consultant who's only ever worked in software. That difference matters.
